Overview

The minisymposium Symbolic and Numerical Methods in (Nonlinear) Algebra is part of JuliaCon 2026, which will take place in Mainz, Germany, 10-15 August 2026. The format of this minisymposium is contributed talks.

Aims and scope

The minisymposium spans both theoretical and applied aspects of computer algebra in Julia. For details, please refer to the Pretalx entry here.

Submit a talk

Talks for this minisymposium are submitted through the official JuliaCon 2026 call for proposals on Pretalx. The deadline for submission is Feb 28th 2026, 23:59 (CET).

Go to JuliaCon 2026 CFP View minisymposium on Pretalx

When submitting your abstract, please indicate that you would like your contribution to be considered for the minisymposium Symbolic and Numerical Methods in (Nonlinear) Algebra.
