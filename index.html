Overview

The minisymposium "Symbolic and Numerical Methods in (Nonlinear) Algebra" is part of JuliaCon 2026, which will take place in Mainz, Germany, 10-15 August 2026. The format of this minisymposium is contributed talks.

Aims and scope

The minisymposium spans both theoretical and applied aspects of computer algebra in Julia. For details, please refer to the Pretalx entry here.
